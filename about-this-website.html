Why I made this website

I am currently an IB student, studying at Emirates International School Jumeirah. I am making this website to try and help others understand the IB curriculum in all its facets. In this website, I will discuss the main subjects within the IB curriculum that are there in the PYP, MYP, and IB programmes. This includes English, but not, for example, computer science. I am creating this qebsite for my personal project, as I feel that it is very important that newcomers to the IB should be able to learn about how it is structured, and should be able to understand the differences between the IB curriculum and other curriculums, as it is very different from other curriculums, especially in the way the exams are laid out, and how they're graded. I decided that I wanted to do this because I have seen many new students to my school struggle in their studies simply because they are not used to the way that the IB works. For example, students coming from ICSE curriculum are focused on memorizing facts, and so the way that IB focuses on skills and the application thereof can often be challenging. So, I wanted to create a website that could help out people, especially those coming from other curriculums, to understand the IB, and how it works, as explained by someone who has experienced most of it. For the record, this website is not officially endorsed by the IB, and is entirely independent. Most information comes from either other IB students, IB teachers, and the official IB website.

Through making this website, I hope that I can help some people who would otherwise be confused or unable to understand how parts of the IB work, and, in doing so, improve my own programming skills.
